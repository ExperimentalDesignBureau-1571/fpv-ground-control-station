# Remote Unit Bracket

The bracket is designed for mounting the ground control station's remote unit on a mast or other supporting structures. The bracket is part of the mechanical integration system of the remote unit. The design is oriented towards field operation and transportation. The general view of the remote unit with additional antennas fixed via the bracket on a mast is shown in the picture.

<img width="800" alt="General view of the remote unit with additional antennas on the mast" src="picture/general_view_of_the_remote_unit_on_the_mast.png" />

The bracket design ensures:
- rapid deployment
- fixing the remote unit
- mounting rods for installing additional antenna systems or equipment
- increasing the antenna installation height
- expanding the antenna configuration according to operating conditions
